Chocolate Fudge

Description

This classic chocolate fudge is smooth, rich, and melts in your mouth. It uses simple pantry ingredients and comes together quickly on the stovetop.

Make a batch for holidays, bake sales, or a late-night sweet tooth— it slices cleanly and keeps well for gifting.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Grease an 8×8 inch pan and set aside.
  2. Whisk sugar, cocoa, and milk in a saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Bring to a gentle boil; cook 5–6 minutes, stirring frequently.
  4. Remove from heat; add butter, vanilla, and salt. Stir until glossy.
  5. Pour into the pan, smooth the top, and let cool completely.
  6. Cut into squares and enjoy.
